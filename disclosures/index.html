How this page works

Three rules govern what shows up here.

RULE 01

Five-day rule

Any new commercial relationship, equity position, or material gift is logged here within five business days of the agreement.

RULE 02

Quarterly review

Every entry is reviewed once per quarter for accuracy. Concluded relationships move to the historical section for 12 months, then drop off.

RULE 03

Over-disclose

When uncertain whether something belongs here, it goes in. The cost of over-disclosing is zero. Under-disclosing compounds.
