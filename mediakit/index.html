— Founder · Editorial voice

James Hicks.

30+ years in enterprise IT. Founded HicksNewMedia in 2010 to make technology make sense for the people who actually decide what gets built. Since then, the agency has grown into a multi-property network covering long-form video, editorial publishing, premium community, and brand strategy.

Editorial philosophy: No Hype. Just the Facts. The work treats audiences as adults — IT decision-makers, technical leaders, and creators who can tell the difference between substance and marketing.

Today HicksNewMedia produces flagship content under the @JamesHicks brand, runs Digital Collective (newsletter + premium community), publishes tek FORUM and Team No Sleep, and partners with brands that want to reach the people who matter most: the ones who sign the purchase orders.
